I have always enjoyed working with computers, that's why I studied Communication & Multimedia Design in Amsterdam. Just after my study I met my girlfriend and we decided to live in Groningen, since she studied there. Moving to Groningen required me find a job, which I did. I went on to work as an online marketeer, launching online campaigns, maintaining various websites and keeping track of the positions in Google our sites were in.

Thanks to the COVID-19 virus though, the company I worked for could no longer keep its employees. That made me rethink my career. I always liked working on websites and had some experience in coding (HTML & CSS and basic PHP), so I decided to pursuit this interest. Because frontend suites me the best, I applied for Codaisseur, thankfully I got accepted and now we're here. Can't wait to get started!
